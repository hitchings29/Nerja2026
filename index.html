Wasting Time Before Check-in

1. Plaza Mayor: Closest to car hire; huge mall with easy stroller paths.

2. Málaga (Muelle Uno): Beautiful flat promenade but high-effort parking.

3. Rincón de la Victoria: Closer to Nerja; lovely flat promenade, quiet.

4. Burriana Beach (Nerja): Get the drive over with; have lunch by the sea.

Verdict: If Aelin is sleeping, drive straight. If awake, Rincón is easiest.

Sunday

Rest & Villa Rituals

First full day to settle in. Focus on a "zero-alarm" morning and enjoying the villa amenities.

Morning

Long breakfast on the terrace and first pool session.

Afternoon

Nap time for Aelin and a relaxed evening at the villa.

Local Option if bored:

The Frigiliana Tourist Train is a great way to see the views of the white village without the steep climb, keeping things easy with the stroller.

Monday

Córdoba

A beautiful 2h 10m drive to one of the most unique buildings in the world. The interior is a forest of arches—cool, quiet, and stroller-friendly.

Must See

The Orange Tree Courtyard (Patio de los Naranjos) is a perfect shaded spot for Aelin to have a break.

Parking

Lot: Paseo de la Victoria

Tuesday

Poolside & Local

Mid-week recharge. Perfect day for a BBQ lunch at the villa.

Nerja Market

Tuesdays, 10am-2pm. Great for local vibe.

Burriana Beach

Afternoon walk; the promenade is wide and flat, perfect for sunset with the stroller.

Wednesday

Ronda & Setenil

Setenil de las Bodegas

A stunning town built into the cliffs. Stick to the riverbed streets for easier stroller access.

Ronda

Head to the Alameda del Tajo park for a flat, shaded walk that leads to incredible balconies overlooking the valley and bridge.

Note: A carrier for Aelin is best for gorge paths.

Parking: Martínez Astein

Thu 30 Apr • Grand Highlight

The Alhambra

The crown jewel of Andalusia. The Nasrid Palaces require a specific time slot—be punctual!

Palaces: Strollers are forbidden inside the palaces; you'll need the carrier.

Gardens: The Generalife gardens are mostly accessible with wheels.

Essential: Do your big grocery shop today! Tomorrow is a holiday.

Plan B (No tickets):

Lazy morning at villa. Afternoon in Frigiliana for a rooftop lunch, or visit the Nerja Caves (naturally cool and easy).

Labor Day

Shops are closed! Use up villa supplies.

The entire day is yours to relax by the pool until departure. Squeeze in one last cold beer!
